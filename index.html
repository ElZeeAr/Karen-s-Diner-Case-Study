Overview

This project analyses the rise and decline of Karen's Diner using regression, time-series forecasting, demand modelling, sentiment analysis, and trend analysis. The core hypothesis: novelty-driven concepts struggle to sustain profitability under inflationary pressure and shifting consumer behaviour.
